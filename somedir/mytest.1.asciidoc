MYTEST(1)
=========


NAME
----
mytest - some test test test


SYNOPSIS
--------
*mytest* 'something...'


DESCRIPTION
-----------
*mytest* lorem ipsum dolor sit amet


OPTIONS
-------
*-h*, *--help*::
	Shows help

*-v*, *--version*::
	Shows version

*-o*, *--output*='FILE'::
	Output to 'FILE'

*-c*, *--cat*::
	Overrides '-o' and '-v'.


EXAMPLES
--------
*mytest file.txt*

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet.  The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.
Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet.  The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.

*mytest file2.txt*

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet.  The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.
Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet.  The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.
